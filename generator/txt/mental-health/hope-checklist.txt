Hope Checklist[html]
QUOTE: Just as despair can come to one only from other human beings, hope, too, can be given to one only by other human beings.  -Elie Wiesel
INSTRUCTIONS: Respect the tragedy of lost hope and work to rebuild.  Use this checklist to help the patient find their sources of hope, comfort, and peace.[/html]
